About Us

Turtlee Inc. Company

Our company is dedicated to raising awareness of the difficult living spaces that marine life currently faces, and raising awareness to protect sea turtles, tortoises, and their natural habitats.

We also hope that the future environment can be a more naturalized human society, which will allow wild animals to coexist, delay or even prevent the extinction of wild species, and preserve the original diversity of the earth. This seems to share the living space, but actually prolongs the life of civilization.
